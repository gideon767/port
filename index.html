Tindog Website

TinDog is a website that connects dog owners with other dog owners in their area to help their furry friends find love and companionship. Users can create a profile for their dog and swipe through other dog profiles to find a potential match. The website also includes resources for dog care and training, as well as a directory of local dog-friendly businesses.
